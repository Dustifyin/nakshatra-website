About Nakshathra Cup Sambrani

We are a family-run sambrani manufacturing unit located in Laxmipura Village, Bangalore. For over 5 years, we've been crafting high-quality sambrani cups with natural ingredients and care. Our goal is to make traditional fragrance affordable and accessible to all while supporting local workers.

We started as a small two-person unit and grew into a trusted brand known for quality, purity, and support.
