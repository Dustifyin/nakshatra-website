About Nakshathra Cup Sambrani

Welcome to Nakshathra Cup Sambrani. We are a family-run sambrani manufacturing unit located in Laxmipura Village, Bannerghatta Main Road, Bangalore, dedicated to bringing pure, traditional fragrance to every home at affordable prices.

For the past 5 years, we have been crafting high-quality cup sambrani using natural resins and herbs, ensuring long-lasting, rich fragrance for your pooja, meditation, and home cleansing needs. We believe purity and tradition should be accessible to everyone, and we take pride in supporting local workers while maintaining consistent quality in every cup.

We Deliver Pure Sambrani Fragrance to Your Home with Care and Quality

We started as a small two-person unit crafting pure cup sambrani for our neighborhood, driven by a dedication to bring traditional fragrance into homes. Over time, we expanded into a larger team, producing a wider range of sambrani variants at affordable prices while maintaining quality and purity.

Whether it's for your daily pooja, meditation, or cleansing your home, we ensure that every cup is made with care, providing a long-lasting, calming fragrance to your space.
