About Us

We are a family-run sambrani manufacturing unit located in Laxmipura Village, Bannerghatta Main Road, Bangalore. For the past 5 years, we have been crafting high-quality cup sambrani using natural resins and herbs, ensuring long-lasting, rich fragrance for your pooja, meditation, and home cleansing needs.

We started as a small two-person unit and expanded into a larger team. Our goal is to make traditional fragrance accessible and affordable while supporting local workers.

Why Choose Us?

  • Friendly Support – We guide and assist you anytime.
  • Fast Delivery – Quick, safe doorstep delivery.
  • Trusted – 5 years of consistent quality.
  • Affordable – Premium fragrance at the best price.
  • Pure & Natural – Made with high-quality ingredients.

About Nakshathra Cup Sambrani

We are a family-run sambrani manufacturing unit located in Laxmipura Village, Bangalore. For over 5 years, we've been crafting high-quality sambrani cups with natural ingredients and care. Our goal is to make traditional fragrance affordable and accessible to all while supporting local workers.
